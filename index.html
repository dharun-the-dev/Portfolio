About Me

I’m a passionate and disciplined Front-End Developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and modern web practices. I come from a Computer Applications background and have been consistently building my skills with one clear goal: to create clean, responsive, and user-focused web experiences.
I believe good front-end development is not just about writing code, but about understanding structure, accessibility, and clarity. That’s why I focus deeply on fundamentals like semantic HTML, layout systems, and reusable components instead of rushing through technologies.
As a fresher, I may not have industry experience yet, but I bring something equally valuable — consistency, curiosity, and a strong willingness to learn and improve every day. I enjoy breaking down problems, learning how things work under the hood, and turning ideas into functional interfaces.
I’m currently looking for an opportunity where I can contribute as a front-end developer, grow under guidance, and add real value to a team through dedication and continuous improvement.
